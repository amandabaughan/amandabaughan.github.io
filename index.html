Amanda Baughan

Hello! I am a third year PhD student in Computer Science & Engineering, advised by Alexis Hiniker. I'm interested in developing informal support for mental health and relationships online using evidence-based interventions. My work draws from a variety of techniques in HCI, data science, and NLP, and is informed by work in psychotherapy and relationship science.

Previously, I worked as a UX Researcher at AI2, studying how users navigate Semantic Scholar. Before that, I spent two years working at Google.

When not working, you can find me in a yoga studio, hiking, snowboarding, or catching up with some good books.
